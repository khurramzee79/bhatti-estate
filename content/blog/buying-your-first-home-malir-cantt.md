## Why Malir Cantt?
Malir Cantonment is widely regarded as one of the safest and most disciplined residential areas in Karachi. For first-time buyers, the security and organized infrastructure are major draws.

### Steps to Buying
1. **Budgeting:** Factor in transfer fees and taxes, not just the property price.
2. **Verification:** Ensure the file is clear. At Bhatti Estate, we handle all verification processes.
3. **Sector Selection:** Sector H and J offer great modern amenities, while older sectors offer larger plot sizes.
